    OUR GAMES

    KITPVP

    KitPvP is a competitive, fast-paced gamemode packed with players! Choose your kit and join the race to the top of the leaderboard. Explore our custom map and test your skills on parkour in secret rooms. Whether you are an archer, gladiator or tactician there is a kit for you. Join the fight today on MonoMC!
